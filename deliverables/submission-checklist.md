# Digital Heroes Submission Checklist

Use this before sending your final form entry.

## Store Build

- Built in Shopify Partner development store (fresh account)
- Theme uploaded and customized from `shopify-theme/`
- Password enabled: `Test@123`

## Required Store Structure

- Home
- Catalog / Collections
- Product pages
- Cart and checkout flow
- About Us
- Contact Us
- FAQ
- Shipping and Returns
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service

## Product Requirements

- 3 to 10 products added
- SEO title and description filled for each product
- Pricing + compare-at pricing configured
- SKU and inventory configured
- Products assigned to collections

## Conversion & Design Requirements

- Hero section with clear CTA
- Featured collections
- Best-selling products
- USP section
- Product highlights
- Testimonials
- Trust badges
- Newsletter signup
- Sticky add-to-cart behavior on product page

## App Stack

- Judge.me Reviews
- Shopify Search & Discovery
- Shopify Bundles
- Shopify Marketplace Connect
- Shopify Subscriptions
- Labeler - Product Labels

## Final QA

- Mobile and desktop checked
- Spacing/alignment polished
- Placeholder content removed
- Links working
- Test checkout path verified
- Discounts and shipping rules tested

## Submission Rule

- Submit only the Shopify store link in the form
- Do not include extra links or unrelated info
